## ALL STANDBY
Unceremoniously, arrival to CRESSIDIUM was not met with any form of resistance, indeed arrival was unanimously quiet and uneventful, even aboard the RIO a lackadaisal atmosphere persist.

Most of the crew and staff are placed on low activitiy duties and standby orders.

However, the same can't be said for the members of the diplomatic delegation and the senior officers. 

Ambassador Bannerjee and Co. are constantly at work, going back and forth from planetary surface to the ship, only breaking time to meet with the senior officers and then promptly returning back to the surface.

## THE DIPLOMATIC SUMMIT, THE NEXT FEW DAYS
The latest diplomatic summit has been extended again as negotiations remain at an impasse, and Ambassador Bannerjee has requested that Captain Farris join them down on the planet to give a speech or two and meet with some of the delegates in the hopes that a new face and fresh perspective might help shift some attitudes. She’s been down there for several days now, and the XO, 1st Lt. Kim, has command.
